Are you involved with the Public Participation Network (PPN) in your community? Development Perspectives is partnering with the Irish Environmental Network to offer free trainings to all Public Participation Networks in Ireland. This is part of the 'Make Ireland Sustainable for All' initiative, funded by the European Commission.

Each training will last for two hours and allow individuals to increase their understanding of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and how you can best integrate them to your work and to your community.

To find out more about this programme, please visit: http://sdgsforall.ie or contact caitriona@ien.ie
