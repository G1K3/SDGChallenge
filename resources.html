RESOURCES & WORKSHOPS

The #SDGchallenge is a 17 month project which focuses on one goal per month with online Information packs, Workshops & Advocacy Training to engage all learning styles.

See Workshop Map

Online Resources

The #SDGchallenge offers participants free access to online resources focusing on each of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Their resources include articles, videos, blogs and much more in one condensed information pack. After learning about each individual goal participants are invited to take one of our many challenges to contribute the commitment of the goals. Are you ready to jump in and take action?

Yes, sign me up!

Workshops

The #SDGchallenge will host 17 interactive workshops across Ireland. Each workshop will last two hours and focus on one specific goal. The aim of each workshop is to make the SDGs a reality in communities across Ireland. The workshops will also allow us to explore how interconnected the SDGs really are and how we can make a change within our communities.
